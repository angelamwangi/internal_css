Margins and padding

Margins=empty space.

Margins are used to control the position of an element relative to those around it.

  • margin-left
  • margin-right
  • margin-top
  • margin-bottom

Short-hand margin property.

margin:top,right,botton,left.

Padding=more background

Used to control the position of content inside the element

Many languages, like French, Spanish, and German, classify nouns as masculine, feminine, or even neuter. This might seem arbitrary, but there are historical and cognitive reasons behind it.

Short-hand padding property.

padding:top,right,botton,left.
