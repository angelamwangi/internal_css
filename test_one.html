Chia pudding

Chia pudding, a delicious treat has gained popularity as a healthy and satisfying dish packed with nutrients.

It is made from chia seeds soaked in liquid, usually milk or a dairy-free alternative, it transforms into a creamy, pudding-like consistency. Chia seeds are known for their high fibre content, omega-3 fatty acids, and protein, making this pudding an excellent choice for a balanced diet.

Simple and Customisable

One of the best things about chia pudding is its versatility. The basic recipe requires only chia seeds, liquid, and a sweetener, but the possibilities for customisation are endless.

You can add fresh fruits, nuts, seeds, or flavourings like vanilla, cinnamon, or cocoa powder to enhance the taste.

Whether enjoyed as a breakfast, snack, or dessert, it can be prepared in advance, making it a convenient option for busy lifestyles.

Health Benefits and Sustainability

Beyond its delicious taste and ease of preparation, chia pudding offers numerous health benefits. It aids digestion, supports heart health, and provides long-lasting energy.

Additionally, chia seeds are environmentally friendly, requiring minimal resources to cultivate.

Choosing chia pudding not only supports personal health but also contributes to a more sustainable food system.
