A Superfood Delight

Chia pudding has gained popularity as a healthy and satisfying dish packed with nutrients. Made from chia seeds soaked in liquid, usually milk or a dairy-free alternative, it transforms into a creamy, pudding-like consistency.

Chia seeds are known for their high fibre content, omega-3 fatty acids, and protein, making this pudding an excellent choice for a balanced diet.

It is naturally gluten-free and can be adapted to suit various dietary needs, including vegan and keto diets.
